About Ittefaq Life

Pioneering Quality in PPRC & PVC Pipe Manufacturing

Our Story

Established in 2005, Ittefaq Life has grown to become one of the leading manufacturers of PPRC and PVC pipes and fittings in the region. What started as a small manufacturing unit has now transformed into a state-of-the-art facility spanning over 50,000 square feet.

For over 18 years, we have been committed to producing high-quality piping solutions that meet international standards. Our journey has been defined by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs.

Today, Ittefaq Life stands as a symbol of reliability and excellence in the piping industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable, and durable piping solutions that contribute to better infrastructure and improved quality of life. We aim to exceed customer expectations through innovation, integrity, and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred piping solutions provider in the region, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Manufacturing Facility

At Ittefaq Life, we take pride in our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with the latest technology and machinery. Our factory operates under strict quality control measures to ensure that every product that leaves our premises meets the highest standards of quality and durability.

From raw material testing to the final product inspection, each step in our manufacturing process is meticulously monitored and controlled by our team of experienced professionals. This ensures consistency in quality and performance across all our products.
